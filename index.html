Why Advocate Low-Carbon?

Climate change is the most severe challenge of our era. Global warming, frequent extreme weather, and melting glaciers all warn us: Earth needs our protection. Low-carbon living is not just an environmental responsibility, but also a healthy, economical, and sustainable lifestyle choice. Reducing carbon emissions individually, when combined, becomes the power to change the world.
